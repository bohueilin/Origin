Certification-as-a-market · the buyer flow

Run a reference check on your agent.

Describe your agent and its least-privilege policy, run it through a deterministic IAM gym, and earn a config-bound readiness credential: the RSL level, the before/after lift, a per-decision breakdown of exactly where your policy over-grants, and a signed Sigil you can download and re-verify offline. The deterministic oracle is the only judge — never an LLM grading an LLM.

Everything runs client-side: your config is not uploaded. A verdict means "reproducible least-privilege behavior under this verifier + this config," never "safe." This is Origin’s synthetic IAM battery — certifying your real agent, against your own policy and environment, is the design-partner path (book below).
