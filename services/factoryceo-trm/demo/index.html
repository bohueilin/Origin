factoryceo-trm
A verifiable RL/RFT environment for autonomous factory operation

The brain that runs the factory while the CEO is away.

A humanoid robot can do the physical work. The hard part is the decision layer: read messy plant context, propose actions, and never ship an infeasible plan. FactoryCEO-TRM compiles chaos into structured state, then a recursive verifier→repair loop drives hard-constraint violations to zero before anything reaches the floor.

brain decides → verifier gates → humanoid executes
