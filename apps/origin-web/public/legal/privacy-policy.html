Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Origin Physical AI (“we”) collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Origin, you agree to this Policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your name and email when you create an account, and authentication data handled by our identity provider.
  • Content you submit — sites, floor plans, and configurations you upload to evaluate.
  • Agent governance data — the grants, session keys, and approval decisions you create, and an append-only audit log of credential-broker activity.
  • Usage and device data — basic logs, IP address, and diagnostics needed to operate and secure the Service.

What we deliberately do not collect

Origin does not store your raw passwords, seed phrases, private keys, or long-lived third-party credentials, and never exposes them to agents. The credential broker holds only references and issues opaque, scoped, time-limited handles. Wallets are linked by public address (and proof of ownership); we hold no signing keys. The audit log stores redacted metadata, not secrets.

How we use information

  • To provide, maintain, and secure the Service and your account.
  • To enforce the scoped, revocable authority you grant to agents and to record an audit trail.
  • To communicate with you about your account, security, and changes to the Service.
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to abuse, fraud, and security incidents.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with: (a) service providers that help us run the Service (for example, hosting, identity, and infrastructure providers) under appropriate confidentiality and security obligations; (b) authorities when required by law; and (c) a successor in the event of a merger or acquisition, subject to this Policy.

Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including least-privilege access, scoped tokens, encryption in transit, append-only audit logging, and fail-closed enforcement at the credential broker. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure.

Data retention and deletion

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service and meet legal obligations. You can delete your credential data from your account’s Danger Zone at any time; the append-only audit log is retained as the record of that deletion. To request removal of your account, contact us.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies and local storage needed to keep you signed in and operate the Service. We do not use non-essential tracking or advertising cookies.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes

We may update this Policy. If we make material changes, we will take reasonable steps to notify you and update the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@origin.ai or through the contact section of our website.
